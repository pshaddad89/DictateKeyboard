DICTATE — THIRD-PARTY DATA ATTRIBUTIONS
=======================================

Dictate bundles and/or downloads language data derived from the following freely
licensed corpora. Only aggregate frequency data (word and word-pair counts) is
redistributed — never the underlying source texts.


1. Word-frequency dictionaries (glide typing, autocorrect, spell check)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Files: ime/dict/<lang>.json  (bundled English; other languages downloaded on demand)

Derived from the OPUS OpenSubtitles frequency lists.
  - OPUS — the open parallel corpus: https://opus.nlpl.eu
  - Please cite: P. Lison & J. Tiedemann (2016), "OpenSubtitles2016: Extracting
    Large Parallel Corpora from Movie and TV Subtitles", LREC 2016.
Casing was restored using the Hunspell dictionaries (github.com/wooorm/dictionaries)
as a build-time oracle only; their word lists are not redistributed.


2. Bigram (word-pair) frequency data for autocorrect context (Tier 2)
---------------------------------------------------------------------
Files: ime/dict/<lang>_bigrams.txt  (bundled English; other languages downloaded on demand)

Derived from the Leipzig Corpora Collection (Wortschatz), used under CC BY.
  - © Universität Leipzig / Sächsische Akademie der Wissenschaften / InfAI
  - https://wortschatz-leipzig.de
  - Please cite: D. Goldhahn, T. Eckart & U. Quasthoff (2012), "Building Large
    Monolingual Dictionaries at the Leipzig Corpora Collection: From 100 to 200
    Languages", LREC 2012.
  - License: Creative Commons Attribution (CC BY) — the downloadable corpus
    packages are CC BY (the online query service is CC BY-NC and is not used here).


3. On-device speech-to-text models (offline transcription)
----------------------------------------------------------
Downloaded on demand into <filesDir>/dictate-models/<model>/ ; no audio ever leaves the device.

  - Whisper (tiny/base/small, multilingual and English) — © OpenAI, MIT License.
    https://github.com/openai/whisper
  - Parakeet TDT 0.6B v3 — © NVIDIA, licensed CC BY 4.0.
    https://huggingface.co/nvidia/parakeet-tdt-0.6b-v3
  - ONNX (int8) exports of both, and the on-device runtime, are from the sherpa-onnx / k2-fsa
    project, Apache-2.0. https://github.com/k2-fsa/sherpa-onnx
  - The Silero VAD model used to segment long recordings — © Silero Team, MIT License.
    https://github.com/snakers4/silero-vad

All of the above permit redistribution; the files are mirrored on the project's own GitHub release
for a stable, project-controlled source.
